Hi! I'm Kara

UX Designer
Phoenix, AZ


I’m a Phoenix, AZ based UX/UI Designer. I have over ten years experience with an extensive design background to include photography, graphic design, web design & development and user experience design. I am passionate about creating user experiences that are memorable, impactful, creative and accomplish the goals of the user and company alike. I am a big-picture thinker and enjoy working on design projects end to end, from ideation all the way to development.

When I’m not UXing you’ll find me exploring new foods around town, at a concert soaking in my favorite music or spending time with family and friends.

As a senior ux designer for ON Semiconductor I lead a team of designers that are responsible for the visual design and front-end development of the company’s website. I love working with a talented team of designers to create a world class experience for all users.
